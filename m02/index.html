Day of the Week Recipe Name Instructions
Monday Chicken & Rice Heat some olive oil in a pan, fry some chicken thighs/breast with spices of your choice, let it cook for a while, let it sit for a few minutes and serve with white rice.
Tuesday Egg Fried Rice Heat olive oil in a pan, add cut onions, spices, and scallions. Cook until golden brown, add 2 eggs and scramble. Then add rice and soy sauce, serve hot.
Wednesday Teriyaki Chicken Add sesame oil to a heated pan, cook chicken until brown. Remove and boil the sauce for 4â€“5 mins. Add chicken back in until cooked through and sticky. Garnish with sesame seeds.
Thursday Toast & Eggs Toast some bread. Add oil to a pan and prepare eggs to your liking.
Friday Fruits Day Cut fruits like apples, bananas, kiwis or any others you like and place them in a bowl.
Saturday Cheat Day, Ice Cream Some good old store-bought ice cream.
Sunday Mashed Potato Boil potatoes, mash with butter, salt, pepper, and a little milk until creamy.
