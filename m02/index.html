Monday Chicken & Rice

Monday: Heat some olive oil in a pan, fry some chicken thighs/breast with spices of your choice, let it cook for a while, let it sit for a few minutes and serve with white rice.

Tuesday: Heat olive oil in a pan, add cut onions, spices, and scallions. Cook until golden brown, add 2 eggs and scramble. Then add rice and soy sauce, serve hot.
