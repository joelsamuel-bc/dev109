Week Starting 04/19/2025
Day of the Week Recipe Name Instructions
Monday Chicken & Rice Heat some olive oil to a pan, fry some chicken thighs/breast with spices of your choice, let it cook for a while, let it sit for a few minutes and serve with white rice.
Tuesday Egg Fried Rice Heat some olive oil to a pan, add in cut onions with spices, scallions and cook until golden brown, later add 2 eggs and let it cook, after that add some rice and soy sauce, serve while hot.
Wednesday Teriyaki Chicken Heat a large pan, add sesame oil to the pan followed by some chicken. Brown the chicken well on all sides then remove from the pan and set aside. Allow the sauce to come to a boil and allow to cook for 4-5 minutes and then add the chicken until its cooked through and the sauce is sticky, finally add some sesame seeds and serve.
Thursday Toast & Eggs Toast some bread of your choice. Add oil to a pan and prepare eggs according to your preference.
Friday Fruits Day Cut fruits like apples, bananas and kiwis or any other of your choice and place them in a bowl.
Saturday Cheat Day, Ice Cream Some good old store bought Ice cream
Sunday Mashed Potato
