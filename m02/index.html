Day of the Week Recipe Name Instructions
Monday Chicken & Rice Heat some olive oil to a pan, fry some chicken thighs/breast with spices of your choice, let it cook for a while, let it sit for a few minutes and serve with white rice.
Tuesday Egg Fried Rice Heat some olive oil to a pan, add in cut onions with spices, scallions and cook until golden brown, later add 2 eggs and let it cook, after that add some rice and soy sauce, serve while hot.
Wednesday Teriyaki Chicken Heat a large pan, add sesame oil to the pan followed by some chicken. Brown the chicken well on all sides then remove from the pan and set aside.
